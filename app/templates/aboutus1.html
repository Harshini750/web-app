What is Heart Disease Risk Prediction?

Heart Risk prediction is a way to find out your risk of heart disease
You can predict how likely you are to have a heart disease in the next five years.

What is the benefit to know the status your heart?

Heart is your valuable organ. It pumps blood to allover your body relentlessly.
If you know your heart condition, you can act accordingly to keep your heart safe and sound.
So act early and act wisely.


What Are uncontrollable risk factors for Heart Disease?

  • sex
  • Older age
  • Family history of heart disease
  • Being postmenopausal
  • Race (African Americans, American Indians, and Mexican Americans are more likely to have heart disease than Caucasians.)

What Are controllable risk factors for Heart Disease?

  • Smoking
  • Uncontrolled high blood pressure
  • Physical inactivity
  • Obesity
  • Uncontrolled diabetes
  • Uncontrolled stress, depression, and anger
  • Poor diet
  • High LDL, or "bad" cholesterol, and low HDL, or "good" cholesterol
  • Alcohol use
