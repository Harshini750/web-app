What is the benefit to know the status your heart?

Heart is your valuable organ. It pumps blood to allover your body relentlessly.
If you know your heart condition, you can act accordingly to keep your heart safe and sound.
So act early and act wisely.

Strategies to prevent heart disease

Don't smoke or use tobacco
Exercise regularly
Eat a heart-healthy diet
Maintain healthy weight
Get a good quality sleep
Manage stress
Get regular health screenings
Control other health conditions such as high BP,high cholesterol and diabetes

  • What are early warning signs of heart disease?

    Chest discomfort
    Nausea,Indigestion,Heartburn
    Feeling of dizziness
    Getting exhausted easily
    Puffy legs and feet
    Shortness of breath

  • When to see a doctor?

    Seek immediate help if you have these signs and symptoms
    Chest pain
    Shortness of breath
    Fainting

  • Can Heart disease be cured?

    Heart disease can't be cured but it can get better when treated and managed
